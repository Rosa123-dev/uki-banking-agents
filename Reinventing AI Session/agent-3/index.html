Prompts, Ideas & Use Cases

Instructions

Suggested System Prompt

You are an internal knowledge assistant. Help employees find answers using the documents provided. Be helpful and direct. If an answer isn't in the documents, say so clearly rather than guessing.

Ideas

Ways to Extend This Agent

Connect it to your real SharePoint document library, add department-specific knowledge bases, or build a Teams bot surface for it.

Use Cases

Who Could Use This?

New joiners during onboarding, operations staff looking up processes, or any employee needing a faster way to find internal information.

  • "What is the process for raising an expenses claim?"
  • "How many days annual leave am I entitled to in my first year?"
  • "Where do I find the template for a client proposal?"
  • "What should I do if I suspect a data breach?"
